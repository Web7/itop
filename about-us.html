    About us

    Synergy of competences

    iTop Distribution is a combination of skills and experience gained on global markets; in two geographically different areas of operations. The company was created by a merger of two companies whose founders have similar competences, but different business histories:

    • since 2005 operating on the CIS and Polish markets,
    • specialising in distribution on the markets of Europe, Asia, Middle East and the USA for 15 years.

    The synergy of know-how and contact databases has helped us develop a business whose European headquarters we have moved to Poland. This is how iTopDistribution.pl was created.

    Our idea

    Our goal is to offer an extensive range of products while maintaining their high availability, affordable prices and professional service. When creating iTopDistribution.pl, we combined our skills with the joy of creating innovative, efficient distribution processes.

    Due to the geographical location of Warsaw - close to Eastern Europe, the Baltic States and selected Western European markets, this is where we moved our operational and logistics centers, thus consolidating both our companies.

    Today, iTop Distribution distributes consumer and lifestyle products, electronics, smartphones, tablets at the B2B level: for re-sellers and commercial customers. We guarantee business transparency, high responsibility towards business partners, employees and the environment. Our business is a sustainable: both on the level of management and exploitation of our planet's resources.
